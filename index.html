The 3 Year, $150,000 Plan

By: Regenerations Garden Services, LLC

December 2016 Draft

The plot of land dictates all elements of an integrated biological systems plan, such as this.

Some examples: The slope is best if downwards towards the equator. Gentle slopes are much easier to work with for our proposed equipment. The surrounding properties have an impact on the chosen property. Farms spray pesticides and fertilizers, industry has byproducts, et cetera. These exterior forces will be a consideration, for example, near Industrial Agriculture Fields, a nonfood biological barrier gives some peace of mind. We also consider climate, proximity to markets, existing biological systems, current and previous land use, and more.

A plan limited to $150,000 has approximately a $50,000 land budget.

Zoning, and the authority of the local governments, are major factors considering the proposed building/housing methods, as well as the hopes to be a living and working community and business.
